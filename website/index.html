Our Services

Cardio (MetCon)

XtraFit’s Metabolic Conditioning combines HIIT, circuits, and interval training to burn fat, boost energy, and improve heart health. Using treadmills, battle ropes, and bodyweight drills, we offer dynamic sessions for all fitness levels. These workouts increase your metabolism and keep calories burning long after the workout ends. Track your progress through our app while training in a motivating, air-conditioned space. Ideal for fat loss, endurance, and time-efficient fitness.

Stretching & Flexibility

Stretching sessions at XtraFit help improve flexibility, reduce muscle stiffness, and prevent injury. Whether post-workout or standalone, our routines focus on full-body relaxation, mobility, and improved posture. Guided by trained instructors, stretches are safe, effective, and adaptable for all age groups. It’s perfect for recovery, stress relief, and maintaining long-term joint health. Combine with strength or cardio for a balanced routine.

Aerobics Classes

Burn calories and boost your mood with high-energy aerobics at XtraFit. Led by certified trainers, our sessions blend dance, step, and cardio routines set to lively music. Great for all fitness levels, aerobics improves heart health, coordination, and stamina. Held in our spacious group studio, it’s low-impact and joint-friendly. Enjoy the fun, community spirit, and full-body results — all while staying motivated.

Personal Training

Get one-on-one fitness guidance with XtraFit’s certified personal trainers. Each program begins with a full fitness assessment, followed by a customized plan for weight loss, strength, or recovery. Trainers help correct form, track progress via app, and offer support beyond workouts — including diet and sleep tips. Personal training ensures faster, safer, and long-lasting results tailored to your lifestyle.

Floor Exercises

Strengthen your core and improve balance with bodyweight floor exercises at XtraFit. Our routines include planks, lunges, push-ups, and crunches — all designed to build mobility, stability, and posture. Ideal for all levels, these workouts can be done anywhere, including at home via our fitness app. Great for functional fitness, fat loss, and rehabilitation.

Strength Training

Unlock your full potential with strength training at XtraFit. Using advanced machines, free weights, and functional gear, we focus on muscle growth, fat loss, and improved posture. Our trainers guide you with proper form and personalized plans. Track your gains with our app and recover faster with massage chairs and steam rooms. Train hard, lift smart, and grow stronger every day.
